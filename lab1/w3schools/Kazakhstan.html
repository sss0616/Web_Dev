Overview

Kazakhstan, located in Central Asia, is the world's largest landlocked country. Known for its vast steppes, beautiful landscapes, and rich history, it offers something for everyone.

Fun Fact: Kazakhstan is home to the Baikonur Cosmodrome, the world's first and largest spaceport.

Culture

The culture of Kazakhstan is a blend of nomadic traditions and modern influences. Some highlights include:

  • Traditional music and instruments like the dombra
  • Delicious cuisine such as beshbarmak
  • Festivals celebrating Nauryz (New Year)
